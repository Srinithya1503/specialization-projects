# Specialization Projects – Domain-Focused Data Analytics Portfolio

Welcome to **Specialization Projects**, a curated collection of domain-specific analytics projects designed to demonstrate **real-world business problem solving** across multiple industries.

This repository focuses on applying **data analytics, visualization, and AI techniques** to solve problems in **Healthcare, HR, and Supply Chain domains**.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this repository is to:

- Build **domain expertise** in key industries  
- Solve **real-world business problems using data**  
- Develop **industry-relevant analytics skills**  
- Strengthen **data storytelling and decision-making abilities**  
- Create a portfolio aligned with **Data Analyst / Data Scientist roles**

---

## 🧩 Domains Covered

### 🏥 Healthcare Analytics
Focuses on analyzing healthcare data to improve patient outcomes, optimize costs, and support clinical decision-making.

**Projects include:**
- 💰 Medical Billing & Cost Analysis  
- 🧑‍⚕️ Patient Length of Stay Analysis  
- 🧪 Clinical Trials Data Analysis  
- 💊 Pharmaceutical Sales Analytics  
- 🔬 New Drug Market Research  

---

### 👥 People & HR Analytics
Focuses on workforce data to improve employee retention, hiring strategies, and organizational efficiency.

**Projects include:**
- 📉 Employee Attrition Analysis  
- 🎯 Job Prediction / Employee Performance Prediction  
- 🧑‍💼 Recruitment Funnel Analytics  
- 📊 Workforce Diversity Analysis  

---

### 🚚 Supply Chain Analytics
Focuses on optimizing logistics, inventory, and operational efficiency.

**Projects include:**
- 📦 Inventory Optimization  
- 🚛 Logistics & Delivery Performance Analysis  
- 📉 Demand Forecasting  
- ⏱️ Order Fulfillment Analysis
